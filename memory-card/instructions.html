Instructions of the game


Game is about to flip the cards and to get match all the cards within the time.

  1. Objective:

    2. The goal of the game is to match all the pairs of cards. Each card has a hidden image or value, and you need to flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs.

  2. Start the game:

    3. When the game begins, all the cards are face down.
    You can flip two cards at a time to try to find a match.
  3. Flipping Cards:

    4. click on a card to flip it over and reveal its image or value.
    Click on another card to flip it over as well.
    If the two flipped cards match, they will remain face up.
    If the two flipped cards don't match, they will flip back over after a short delay.
  4. Winning the Game:

    5. The game is won when all pairs of cards have been matched.
    The number of attempts or the time taken may be shown on the screen, depending on how the game is designed.
  5. Restarting the Game:

    6. After you finish or want to restart, you can click a "Restart" button to shuffle the cards and start a new game.
